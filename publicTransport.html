Public transport info


Public transport in Cincinnati has not been majorly affected by the Covid-19 pandemic. All bus, tram, and metro lines are still operating, however there are certain recommendations that citizens should follow when using public transport, such as:

  • Wearing a face mask (mandatory)

  • Not sitting next to someone if there are seats available that no one is sitting next to

  • Not touching surfaces like hand rails unless absolutely necessary (for example, in the case where you need to stand up)

  • Setting up contactless payment on your phone or credit card

  • Not using public transport if you feel unwell or begin to develop symptoms of the virus

Additionally, to further help prevent the spread of the virus, all public transport companies in Cincinnati are undergoing daily vehicle cleaning for all vehicles in use. This includes:

  • Using an EPA-rated (Environmental Protection Agency) Tier 1 disinfectant that gets rid of the harmful virus on all major bus surfaces such as seats and hand-rails

  • Installing hand-sanitizing stations in all garages and office, for use by vehicle operators and employees
