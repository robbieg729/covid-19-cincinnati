Interactive map

Below is a map of Cincinnati, showing the 53 different neighborhoods. Click on a neighborhood to see its current Covid-19 data, and click on the 'Advanced data' button to see a further breakdown of cases. The 'Show services' button, when clicked, will show the locations of hospitals, testing sites, and some of the top stores and bars in the city, with additional information about them presented when their location is clicked.
